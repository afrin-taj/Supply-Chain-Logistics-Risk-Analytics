# 🚚 Supply Chain Logistics & Risk Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Supply Chain Logistics & Risk Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end business intelligence project developed using **MySQL, SQL, DAX, and Power BI**. The dashboard provides insights into supply chain performance, logistics efficiency, delivery risks, and strategic business recommendations. It enables stakeholders to monitor operations, identify bottlenecks, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Features

* Executive Overview Dashboard
* Logistics Performance Analysis
* Risk Analytics and Profitability Insights
* Business Insights and Strategic Recommendations
* Interactive Filters and Drill-Down Analysis
* Advanced Visualizations including Sankey Diagram and Decomposition Tree

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance.

#### KPIs

* Total Orders
* Total Sales
* Average Profit
* Average Shipping Days
* Top Market

#### Visuals

* Sales by Market
* Top Product Categories
* Delivery Status Analysis
* Customer Segment Performance

---

### 2️⃣ Logistics Performance

Analyzes shipping efficiency and operational bottlenecks.

#### KPIs

* Average Shipping Days
* Delayed Orders
* Late Delivery Risk
* Fastest Shipping Mode

#### Visuals

* Shipping Mode Efficiency
* Order Status Flow (Sankey Diagram)
* Shipping Volume vs Delivery Time
* Regional Performance Analysis

---

### 3️⃣ Risk Analytics

Evaluates delivery risks and profitability across regions and categories.

#### KPIs

* High Risk Orders
* Late Delivery Risk %
* Average Profit

#### Visuals

* Risk by Market
* Profitability Analysis
* Category Profit Contribution
* Decomposition Tree Analysis

---

### 4️⃣ Business Insights & Recommendations

Provides strategic insights and actionable recommendations.

#### Key Insights

* Market Leadership Analysis
* Customer Segment Insights
* Shipping Pattern Analysis
* Product Performance Insights

#### Strategic Recommendations

* Improve Standard Class Delivery
* Focus on High-Revenue Markets
* Prioritize Top Categories
* Reduce Delivery Risk
* Implement Risk Mitigation Strategies
